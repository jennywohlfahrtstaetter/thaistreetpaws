How to adopt

If you see a dog that you're interested in adopting, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Fill in the application form and return.
  2. If your application passes the first round, you will be scheduled for a Skype / FaceTime interview.
  3. If your application is approved, we will begin looking for a flight angel from Bangkok to an airport near you.
  4. Once the flight angel is secured, travel necessities will be bought and all paperwork completed<./li>
  5. On their arrival, you will meet the flight angel and your dog at the airport.

If you're interested in one of the dogs please send a message so we can start the process.

What's the cost?

There is no adoption fee when you adopt a Thai Street Paws dog but you will need to pay for the dog's travel costs.

These include the following:

  • IATA approved travel crate
  • Microchip
  • Paperwork fees for export and import of dog
  • Flight from Hat Yai to Bangkok
  • Flight from Bangkok to your nearest airport (this cost is significantly reduced when the dog flies with a flight angel)
  • Lodging for the dog and Tamara in Bangkok

Costs vary relative to the size of the dog, as well as the airline. Typically, costs range between US$350 to US$500.
