Ways to get involved...

Sponsor a dog

Help pay for veterinary care and food for one of our dogs - weekly, monthly or one-off.

Sponsor

Volunteer at the sanctuary

We welcome volunteers at Thai Street Paws Rescue who are looking for a fulfilling way to give back by working with the street dogs of Songkhla.

Please remember volunteering at the rescue is a “working” holiday. Send us a message for details.

Contact us

Be a flight volunteer

Flying from Thailand? You can help dogs fly to their forever homes by being a flight volunteer. We arrange everything. Contact us for details.

Reach out to us

Make a donation

Help us continue to provide life-saving care for street dogs in Thailand. Your donation directly supports their medical treatment, food, and daily needs. Whether it's a one-time gift or a monthly contribution, your generosity makes a real difference in the lives of the dogs we rescue.

Donate

Spread the word

Every share counts! By telling others about our mission, you can help us find more supporters, volunteers, and forever homes for the dogs in our care.

Spread the word on social media or simply share our story with friends and family. Together, we can build a community of compassionate people dedicated to making a difference.
